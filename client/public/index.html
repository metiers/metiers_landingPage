What Metiers has to Offer

Search for Jobs

Ability to search for job opportunities through third-party sources and save them to your dashboard.

Enter Job Leads

Manually enter and save job leads you may know of, whether it is online or through someone in your network.

Personal Dashboard

View top ranking saved jobs in card form or in detailed list form filter based on progress, such as "Will Apply", "Applied" and "Follow Up".

Notifications

Create timely in-app notifications to help you keep track of important upcoming events, such as phone or on-site interviews. Text notifications coming soon.

History Log & Progress Tracking

View a historical log of all events pertaining to a single job. Ability to keep track and update progress for each individually saved job.

Job Notes

Jot down any notes that you may need such as reminders or tips for any of your saved jobs.
